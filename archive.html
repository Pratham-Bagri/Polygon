Problem Archive

A categorized collection of past problem sets, proofs, and challenges.

Calculus

POTW-041

Prove the Basel problem, showing that: $$ \sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6} $$

Number Theory

POTW-040

Prove that there are infinitely many primes $p$ such that: $$ p \equiv 1 \pmod 4 $$

Linear Algebra

POTW-039

Let $A$ and $B$ be $n \times n$ matrices. Prove that: $$ \text{tr}(AB) = \text{tr}(BA) $$
