Theorem of the Week

Fermat's Little Theorem

NUMBER THEORY
If $p$ is a prime number, then for any integer $a$:
$$ a^p \equiv a \pmod p $$

Basis for modern RSA cryptography.

Problem of the Week

Functional Equation

Show that there are no continuous functions $f$, $g$ and $h$ defined on $\mathbb{R}$ satisfying
$$ h(f(x)+g(y)) = xy \quad\forall x, y \in \mathbb{R}$$
